Welcome!


I’m a high school computer science teacher who teaches intro level video game design, honors & AP level comp sci courses, and lastly intro and advanced web design. JavaScript, Java, Python, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap are primarily what is covered in my courses but opportunites to explore other topics in comp sci is a choice I give my students.

I’m happiest when solving a problem, exploring a new concept and connecting students to the possibilities of computer science. Over the course of my teaching career I have had the chance to bring my skills of design, programming and passion for learning to my job every single day.

When approached by family or friends I find the time to help with building custom websites using the tools I teach my students. I find creating a website both educational and rewarding.

Boredem is something I have never experienced. I am always learning.
